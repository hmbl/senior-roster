Senior Roster Application

This application is called 'Senior Roster' and is available to access in the console of this webpage.

At my current job I am required to monitor staff and their breaks using a roster. This application will help me to keep track of staff members and tracking information regarding them. It is pure JavaScript, using loops, arrays, functions, and time conversion methods to calculate a series of information.
Upon taking an array of information including name, rostered start time and shift length, the app will:

  • Calculate users approximate break times on rostered shift length
  • Return data relating to an hour of the day, whether staff need breaks, are arriving or leaving
  • Records times on when breaks were taken and what length they were
  • Tallies any sick staff and displays them in a list
